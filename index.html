Fit Testing

Current State

Mailed FIT is currently implemented in four sites and has shifted to a VISN-wide program with a memorandum issued to all VISNs on December 20, 2022. Network Directors must start implementation of mailed FIT program in two or more facilities per VISN within 12 months of memo release. VISN Mailed FIT Program must include components as outlined in the memorandum. Mailed FIT programs are encouraged to participate in ongoing quality improvement efforts to boost screening participation and share lessons learned with the National Colorectal Cancer Screening Program (NCSP) and other facilities and VISNs.

Importance of CRC Screening Programs

CRC is the second leading cause of cancer deaths in the United States. The likelihood of surviving five years after a CRC diagnosis is 90% when canceris detected in early stages, 69% when detected in a regional stage, and 8% for when detected at an advanced stage. CRC screening can be achieved through various screening tests, such as annual FIT, colonoscopy every 10 years, and sigmoidoscopy every 5 years. High screening rates are the best way to not only increase early detection, but also to reduce the incidence of CRC as pre-cancerous polyps are identified and removed.

My Role

Mailed FIT Batch Ordering PowerShell Script The Mailed FIT Batch Order PowerShell (PS) script automates laboratory test ordering within the VistA electronic health record system. The script reads data from an Excel file containing patient and test information, establishes a secure shell (SSH) connection with the VistA server, and sends commands to the server to create the lab test order.
